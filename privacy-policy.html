PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At TubeForge, your privacy is our priority. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not store personal identification information. The information we collect is minimal and strictly for operational purposes:

  • Anonymous Usage Data: Pages visited, tools used, and basic analytics (like browser type and device) to improve our website.
  • Contact Information: If you use our contact form, we collect your name and message to respond to your inquiry. This data is kept secure and never shared.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enable core website functionality and serve relevant advertisements.

2. How We Use Your Information

The anonymous data and cookies we collect are used to:

  • Improve and optimize the performance of our YouTube tools.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to create better features.
  • Respond to your feedback, bug reports, or feature requests.
  • Show personalized and non-personalized advertisements via Google AdSense.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on TubeForge, which helps us keep our tools 100% free. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your browsing history. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our tools interact with third-party APIs to function effectively:

  • YouTube API / Public Data: We fetch public thumbnails and metadata directly from YouTube's public servers. We do not access private video data.
  • AI Generation: We may use third-party AI APIs (like Pollinations.ai) to generate tags and titles. No user-identifiable data is sent to these APIs, only the text prompts.
  • Google Fonts: Used to render the text on our website visually.

5. Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them for:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by AdSense to deliver relevant ads and track ad performance.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect how certain features of the site work.

6. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption to ensure that your connection to our website is secure. Since we do not process payments, passwords, or store personal user accounts, the risk of data breaches is extremely low. Any data processed by our tools happens instantly in your browser or via secure API calls.

7. Children's Privacy

TubeForge is intended for general audiences and YouTube creators. Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the revised "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us. You can contact us via the contact form on our Homepage or directly at our website: TubeForge.
